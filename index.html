About Fradesh

Fradesh Bakery & Table Water is a proudly Nigerian company dedicated to producing premium quality bread and purified drinking water for homes, businesses, schools, events, and communities. Since our establishment, we have built our reputation on quality, freshness, hygiene, and dependable customer service. Every loaf of bread is baked with carefully selected ingredients, while our table water is produced using modern purification processes that meet high safety and quality standards. Our mission is simple, to provide families and businesses with fresh bread and clean drinking water they can trust every day. Whether you need daily household supplies, bulk corporate deliveries, or event orders, Fradesh is committed to delivering excellence, convenience, and value with every order.
